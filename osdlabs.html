OSDLabs
I established the Open Source Development Labs (OSDLabs) to foster open source development and innovation at BITS Goa.
At OSDLabs, we set up open-source projects to develop campus infrastructure and spread technical culture and open-source awareness in college.
We also encourage students to contribute to the open-source community at large and hold workshops and tutorials to teach fundamentals of engineering and open-source.
